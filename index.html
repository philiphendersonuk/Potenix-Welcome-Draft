Welcome to Potenix

Potenix is a new kind of biotech company.

We combine synthetic biology, compact hardware and data-driven optimisation to turn unavoidable food scraps into on-site power.

Instead of paying for food waste to be hauled away, our system keeps the energy where it’s needed, cuts carbon along the chain, and gives organisations clear, auditable performance data.

This module will walk you through what we do, how it works, and why it matters.

Why Food Waste Is a Missed Energy Opportunity

Every year, businesses throw away enough food to represent huge amounts of unused energy.

Buffet leftovers, uneaten hospital meals, processing off-cuts, catering waste on production sets — most of it goes into bins, then lorries, then landfill or centralised treatment.

That means:

  • High waste costs
  • Emissions at every stage
  • Valuable energy lost
